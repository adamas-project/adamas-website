ADAMAS (All Decisions And Memory Archive System) is a product of Falcon Intelligence Group LLC, built on the Hermes AI agent. Hermes is a third-party tool and the property of its respective owner.

Pricing shown is indicative and may vary with scope. The Clarity Audit fee is credited toward your build if you proceed. Client hardware (e.g., Mac Mini M4) is purchased and owned by the client.
