Privacy Policy

Falcon Intelligence Group LLC · Last updated June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Falcon Intelligence Group LLC (“Falcon Intelligence Group,” “we,” “us”) handles information in connection with the ADAMAS website at adamas-project.com and the ADAMAS product and services.

1. Our local-first principle

ADAMAS is built local-first. Your company’s decision ledger and vault run on hardware you own. We do not collect, store, or transmit the contents of your vault to our servers or to third-party clouds. Optional hybrid cloud processing is used only for specific tasks you explicitly approve, and only for the duration of that task.

2. Information we collect from this website

This website is intentionally minimal. It does not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party analytics that profile you. The only personal information we receive is what you choose to send us — for example, when you email us or request a Clarity Audit. That typically includes your name, email address, company, and anything you write to us.

3. How we use information

  • To respond to your enquiry and schedule a Clarity Audit or call.
  • To deliver, support, and improve our services for you.
  • To send information you have requested. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. We share information only with service providers who help us operate (e.g., email and scheduling), under confidentiality obligations, or where required by law.

5. Retention

We keep enquiry and client information only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law, then delete or anonymise it.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under the EU/EEA GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these, contact us at m@adamas-project.com.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but our local-first architecture is designed to minimise the data that ever leaves your control.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
