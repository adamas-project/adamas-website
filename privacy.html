1Who we are & scope

Falcon Intelligence Group LLC is the entity responsible for the Services and is the controller of personal information described in this Policy unless stated otherwise. This Policy applies to personal information we process through the Site, our sales and onboarding process, our client and supplier relationships, and our business communications. It does not apply to third-party websites or services we do not control, or to information you maintain solely within your own local ADAMAS environment.

2Information we collect

We aim to collect the minimum personal information necessary. Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect the following categories:

Information you provide to us

  • Identity & contact details — such as your name, business email address, telephone number, company name, role, and country.
  • Enquiry & engagement information — the content of your messages, the details you share when you request a Clarity Audit or a call, and information exchanged during scoping, onboarding, and delivery.
  • Commercial information — records of services discussed or purchased, proposals, and billing/contact information for invoicing (we do not collect payment-card numbers through the Site).

Information collected automatically

  • Technical & server-log data — when you visit the Site, our hosting provider may automatically process limited technical data such as your IP address, browser type, device and operating system, referring page, and the date/time of your request, for security, reliability, and abuse-prevention purposes.
  • The Site does not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies and does not run third-party advertising or profiling analytics (see Cookies & tracking).

Information from third parties

  • Referrals & professional networks — if a mutual contact introduces you, or you reach us through a professional network (e.g., LinkedIn), we may receive your name, company, and contact details.
  • Service providers — providers that support our outreach, scheduling, or CRM may supply business-contact information used to communicate with you.

We do not intentionally collect special categories of personal data (such as health, biometric, or government-identifier data) through the Site, and we ask that you not send such information to us.

3Sources of information

We obtain personal information directly from you (when you contact us or engage our Services), automatically from your use of the Site, and occasionally from third parties such as referrers, publicly available professional sources, and our service providers.

4How we use information

We use personal information to:

  • respond to your enquiries and provide the information or resources you request;
  • schedule and conduct calls, the Clarity Audit, and related consultations;
  • provide, deliver, support, maintain, and improve the Services;
  • manage our client, supplier, and prospect relationships, including contracts and invoicing;
  • communicate with you about your enquiry, engagement, or service-related matters;
  • operate, secure, and improve the Site (including diagnosing problems and preventing abuse);
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements; and
  • establish, exercise, or defend legal claims and protect our rights, property, and safety and those of others.

We do not use your personal information for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects about you, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

5Legal bases for processing

Where the EU/UK GDPR or similar laws apply, we rely on one or more of the following legal bases: (a) your consent; (b) the performance of a contract with you or steps taken at your request before entering a contract; (c) our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and growing our business in a balanced and proportionate way; and (d) compliance with a legal obligation. Where we rely on consent, you may withdraw it at any time (see Your privacy rights).

6How we disclose information

We do not sell your personal information. We disclose personal information only as described below:

  • Service providers (processors). Trusted vendors who process information on our behalf and under contract. Specifically:
    • Netlify — hosts the Site and receives the form submissions you send through it (contact, Clarity Audit, guide downloads, waitlist).
    • Plausible Analytics — cookieless, EU-hosted website analytics; processes anonymous, aggregated page-view data only.
    • Google — call scheduling via Google Calendar booking (the details you enter when booking). The Site does not load remote Google Fonts; fonts are served from our own host so no font request is sent to Google before you book.
    • Business email provider — stores and transmits correspondence you send to our mailboxes.
    These providers may only use the information to provide services to us.
  • Professional advisors. Lawyers, accountants, auditors, and insurers, where reasonably necessary.
  • Legal, compliance & safety. Where required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Falcon Intelligence Group, our clients, or others.
  • Business transfers. In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganisation, or sale of assets, subject to appropriate confidentiality protections.
  • With your direction or consent. When you ask us to share information or otherwise agree to its disclosure.

7We do not sell your personal information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not “share” it for cross-context behavioural advertising, as those terms are defined under applicable U.S. state privacy laws. We have not done so in the preceding twelve months.

8Cookies & tracking technologies

This Site sets no tracking cookies. It does not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or third-party profiling analytics, and therefore shows no cookie banner — there is nothing to consent to. Any cookies or local-storage values we use are strictly necessary to operate the Site (for example, remembering your selected colour theme). Our analytics run on Plausible, a cookieless, EU-hosted, privacy-first service that collects no personal data and stores no identifiers in your browser. Because we do not engage in cross-site tracking, we treat browser “Do Not Track” and Global Privacy Control signals consistently with this no-tracking approach.
