How connectors work

  • Read-only. Connectors use read-scoped credentials. ADAMAS never writes to, posts in, or modifies your tools.
  • Inbound. Data is pulled onto your machine on a schedule you control. Nothing is pushed out.
  • Local processing. Hermes evaluates everything on your hardware — the source material never transits a vendor cloud. See Security.

What gets ingested — and what doesn't

You choose sources and scopes during the build. Common pattern: company channels yes, DMs no; deal pipelines yes, personal inboxes only for the founder. Scopes are revisited at every quarterly review.

ADAMAS (All Decisions And Memory Archive System) is a product of Falcon Intelligence Group LLC, built on the Hermes AI agent. Hermes is a third-party tool and the property of its respective owner.

Local-first by design: your vault and decision ledger run on hardware you own. ADAMAS does not send your company's knowledge to third-party clouds — optional hybrid cloud routing is used only for tasks you explicitly approve.

Pricing shown is indicative and may vary with scope. The Clarity Audit fee is credited toward your build if you proceed. Client hardware (e.g., Mac Mini M4) is purchased and owned by the client.
