# 20_areas/ — ongoing responsibilities

**Areas** are things you maintain with no end state (unlike projects, which finish).
Each area is a folder with an `index.md` you keep current.

Suggested areas (create the ones you want):

- `finance/` — money in/out, runway, decisions about money (`MNY-` ledger ids)
- `network/` — people you know (`person` template), warm/cold, who to keep in touch with
- `learning/` — your standing learning practice (feeds `personal/education`)
- `health/` — standing habits and routines
- `ops/` — your own operations (tools, subscriptions, systems)

When an area produces a decision worth keeping, log it with `/brain-decision` and link
it from the area's `index.md`.
