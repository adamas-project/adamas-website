The decision-memory layer for Google Workspace

So much of your company's thinking happens in Google Workspace — a decision in a Doc, an approval over Gmail, a call in Meet. The reasoning is all there; it's just scattered across four products and a thousand files.

ADAMAS is the decision ledger that connects to Google Workspace and captures the why behind every decision made there — structuring it into a permanent, searchable record your whole company can use. It doesn't replace Google Workspace; it gives it a memory.

Where decisions disappear in Google Workspace

A decision approved in a Gmail thread, debated in a Doc, or made on a Meet call is real and reasoned — but it isn't structured or linked, so it can't be found when it matters months later.

Google Workspace is the richest source of your company's why — and the easiest place for it to get lost.

How ADAMAS connects to Google Workspace

ADAMAS plugs into Google Workspace and continuously reads the places where decisions and their reasoning live:

  • Google Docs decisions, comments and edit history
  • Gmail threads and approvals where calls get made
  • Drive files that hold the context
  • Google Meet recordings and transcripts
Example

A budget is approved in a Gmail thread. ADAMAS captures the decision, reads the thread for the reasoning and who approved it, and links it to the related Doc — so the approval is findable forever, not buried in an inbox.

Google Workspace + ADAMAS — FAQ

Does ADAMAS replace Google Workspace?

No. You keep working in Docs, Gmail, Drive and Meet; ADAMAS reads the decisions made across them and structures them into one decision ledger.

How does the Google Workspace integration work?

Through Google's APIs — ADAMAS connects to the Docs, Gmail, Drive and Meet data you authorise, identifies decisions and their reasoning, and writes structured entries.

Is our content sent to a third-party cloud?

No. ADAMAS is local-first and runs on hardware you own; external processing only happens for tasks you explicitly approve.

Can it combine Workspace with our CRM?

Yes — ADAMAS unifies reasoning from Google Workspace, your CRM, chat and project tools into one decision ledger.
