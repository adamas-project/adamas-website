The decision-memory layer for Pipedrive

Pipedrive is built around the pipeline — deals move crisply from stage to stage. But the judgement behind each move (why this deal advanced, why that one stalled, why you walked away) lives in activities and notes, then fades.

ADAMAS is the decision ledger that connects to Pipedrive and captures the why behind every decision made there — structuring it into a permanent, searchable record your whole company can use. It doesn't replace Pipedrive; it gives it a memory.

Where decisions disappear in Pipedrive

A Pipedrive stage change is a decision with a reason behind it. That reason is rarely captured in a structured way, so your pipeline becomes a record of motion without memory.

When you review why a quarter went the way it did, you're reconstructing reasoning from fragments — instead of reading a clean record of the calls you actually made.

How ADAMAS connects to Pipedrive

ADAMAS plugs into Pipedrive and continuously reads the places where decisions and their reasoning live:

  • Deal stage changes and the person who moved them
  • Activities and notes — where the reasoning is captured
  • Won / lost outcomes, structured and linked
  • Organization and contact context for every decision
Example

A deal stalls and slips two stages back. ADAMAS records the decision, pulls the note explaining why (“champion left, no budget owner”), and links it to similar stalls — so you can see the pattern behind your pipeline, not just its current shape.

Pipedrive + ADAMAS — FAQ

Does ADAMAS replace Pipedrive?

No — it sits on top. Pipedrive stays your pipeline CRM; ADAMAS captures the reasoning behind every stage move and builds a decision ledger from it.

How does it connect to Pipedrive?

Via Pipedrive's API and webhooks — ADAMAS reads deal stage changes, activities and notes, then writes a structured decision entry.

Where does the reasoning get stored?

In your local-first ADAMAS ledger, on hardware you own. Nothing goes to a third-party cloud without your explicit approval.

Can it combine Pipedrive with our other tools?

Yes. ADAMAS unifies decisions from Pipedrive, comms, meetings, drives and docs into one ledger.
